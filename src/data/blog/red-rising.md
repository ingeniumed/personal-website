[Red Rising](https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/15839976-red-rising) is a book I first read all the way back in 2016 at the recommendation of a colleague of mine. He and I had been talking about our love for sci-fi TV shows and how I had just gotten a Kindle to start reading once more. He mentioned this book and said it was a must read. I got back to him a month later confirming that all 3 books were indeed amazing, and that I finished them all in rapid succession. With the way things had been left at the end of Morning Star, I thought it was done. That ending was beautiful and full of hope.

Nearly 9 years later, my brother asked me if I had heard of Red Rising and I said I read all 3 books nearly 10 years ago. He mentioned that there were 6 books in total, and that the next 3 books were definitely worth checking. So after I finished [Dungeon Crawler Carl](/posts/dungeon-crawler-carl), I decided to jump right back into it. I admit I needed to have Claude give me a quick refresher of what had happened in the trilogy.

Well, after finishing Light Bringer recently I can wholeheartedly say that books 4-6 are just as good as books 1-3. They do a wonderful job of worldbuilding yet again, and showing how people can change through the experiences they go through. I'm in a different place in life now, and these books touched a different nerve in me compared to when I first read them. I felt this raw emotion for Darrow, and what he's going through in life as a family man.

I'm super excited for Red God and I'd really recommend this series to anyone.

Bonus: If you are a fan of Graphic Audio, they have books 1-5 and half of book 6 recorded. I'd recommend them over the regular audio book as their production quality is awesome.
